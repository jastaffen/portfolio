Jack Staffen is a software engineer, musician, and composer based in New York, New York.

JavaScript, React, React Native, CSS, HTML, Redux, Ruby on Rails, Ruby, SQL, Sinatra. A recent graduate of Flatiron School's Immersive Web Development Program.

Multi-instrumentalist, sound engineer, composer, and producer. Jack co-founded Motel Home Music and has written music for other artists, collaborated with companies including HBO, Vogue, FXX, Ralph Lauren, and Glossier and has scored and sound designed/edited independent films.
